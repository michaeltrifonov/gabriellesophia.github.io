  <div class="container">
    <div class="cs-section-grid">
      <div class="cs-sidebar">
        <h2 class="cs-sidebar-title">System Audit</h2>
      </div>
      <div class="cs-content">
        <h3 class="cs-subtitle">Comprehensive analysis</h3>
        <p class="cs-large-text">
          I conducted a thorough audit of Egret's current functionality, examining user flows, information architecture, and system interactions. The goal was to identify not just pain points, but opportunities for fundamental improvement.
        </p>
        
        <h3 class="cs-subtitle" style="margin-top: 48px;">Key insights from the audit</h3>
        <div class="cs-findings">
          <div class="cs-finding">
            <div class="cs-finding-number">01</div>
            <div class="cs-finding-content">
              <h4>Information Silos</h4>
              <p>Related data scattered across multiple screens, requiring users to maintain mental maps of where information lived.</p>
            </div>
          </div>
          
          <div class="cs-finding">
            <div class="cs-finding-number">02</div>
            <div class="cs-finding-content">
              <h4>Workflow Tunnel Vision</h4>
              <p>Task-focused design prevented users from seeing how their actions impacted the broader operational plan.</p>
            </div>
          </div>
          
          <div class="cs-finding">
            <div class="cs-finding-number">03</div>
            <div class="cs-finding-content">
              <h4>Duplicate Data Entry</h4>
              <p>Same information required in multiple workflows, leading to inconsistencies and user frustration.</p>
            </div>
          </div>
          
          <div class="cs-finding">
            <div class="cs-finding-number">04</div>
            <div class="cs-finding-content">
              <h4>Limited Visibility</h4>
              <p>No unified view of program health, resource allocation, or planning status across the organization.</p>
            </div>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</section>
