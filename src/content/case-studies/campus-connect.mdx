  <div class="container">
    <div class="cs-section-grid">
      <div class="cs-sidebar">
        <h2 class="cs-sidebar-title">Reflections</h2>
      </div>
      <div class="cs-content">
        <p class="cs-large-text">
          Campus Connect demonstrates how thoughtful UX design can transform complex technical data into actionable insights. By prioritizing visual clarity and progressive disclosure, we created a tool that empowers network administrators to maintain system reliability while reducing cognitive load.
        </p>
        
        <h3 class="cs-subtitle">Key Learnings</h3>
        
        <div class="cs-learnings">
          <div class="cs-learning">
            <h4>Visual Hierarchy is Critical</h4>
            <p>In high-stress situations, clear visual indicators can mean the difference between quick resolution and extended downtime. Our color-coded alert system proved essential for rapid issue identification.</p>
          </div>
          
          <div class="cs-learning">
            <h4>Context Reduces Complexity</h4>
            <p>By anchoring technical data to physical locations on a map, we made abstract network concepts more tangible and easier to understand for administrators.</p>
          </div>
