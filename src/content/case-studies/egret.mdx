Focus groups and surveys revealed that users were struggling with fragmented workflows, leading to low adoption rates and jeopardizing the platform's future.

With these established problem areas in hand, my focus was on executing a UX redesign for this critical operations planning tool which had been suffering from poor adoption and was at risk of deprecation. The goal was to specifically address the three key improvement areas that had emerged from the initial feedback: simplicity, usability, and data traceability.</p>
